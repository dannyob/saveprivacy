STOP THE SPIES

Prelaunch

Who are the spies? The Anti-Terrorism laws which are currently being developed contain some questionable clauses about your digital privacy. This website hopes to provide you with the information you need to be informed and tools to contact your representatives to let them know how you feel about the issue.

What needs to be done?

For at least one week, we need people visiting the campaign website and contacting their representatives in every way possible

Individuals

Don't be dis-illusioned by online campaigns, in the past we have had legislators who weren't even aware of issues until their constituents raised their concerns.
