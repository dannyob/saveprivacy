Who are the spies? The Anti-Terrorism laws which are currently being developed contain some questionable clauses about your digital privacy. This website hopes to provide you with the information you need to be informed and tools to contact your representatives to let them know how you feel about the issue.

Recently the current Australian government has announced they are making plans to bring in new laws which undermine citizens internet privacy.


The new laws are a part of many ammendments made to the Anti-Terrorism bill and focus on stricter retention of citizens browsing habits.


You can read more about the plans here. (Also includes a funny interview with the Attorney General)


Stop The Spies is a grass-roots campaign to raise awareness and pressure policy makers into making better decisions about citizen privacy.


We are currently in the planning stages, and if you are interested in participating, please join in the discussion on GitHub.
